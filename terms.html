2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

AIC is an autonomous incident triage platform that:

  • Analyzes production alerts from AWS CloudWatch/EventBridge
  • Classifies incident severity (P0-P3)
  • Executes AWS diagnostic commands automatically
  • Posts structured notifications to Slack or Microsoft Teams
  • Learns from historical incidents via self-learning RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

The Service runs on AWS infrastructure in the us-east-1 region. Additional regions may be added in the future.
