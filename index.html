// How it works

Alert in.

AIC ingests alerts from CloudWatch, EventBridge, PagerDuty, or any webhook. It classifies severity, runs AWS diagnostics, discovers blast radius, and posts a structured resolution card to Slack — all before a human would even open the alert.
